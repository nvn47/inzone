About Us

Inzone Interior Architects

We, Inzone the interior architects, create beautiful modern interior & Architecture, exploring the power of simple form & spaces specific to place & client desire. Since its founding in 2009, Inzone interior architects have designed numerous residential and commercial spaces throughout Globe.

Inzone interior architects offer a designers sense of artistry supported by practical knowledge gained from years of direct hands-on building experience. The team respectfully guides the client toward what could and what should happen in the space, so they are both pleased and surprised by the result.

How We Do

At InZone we combine the principles and professionalism of architecture with the creativity and flair of interior design. All work, from concept to the final drawings, is beautifully presented to make it easy for our clients to visualize the finished result.

RESIDENTIAL

InZone works on refurnishments and renovations, as well as new builds. Our interior design and Architecture services include:
- Plan and Elevation
- Electrical and Plumbing Drawings
- Space planning - Design of renovations and small extensions
- Kitchens and laundries - Bathrooms
- Custom joinery design for bookshelves, wall units, libraries, studies, offices etc
- Furniture specifications and layouts - Specification of finishes, fixtures, fittings
- Lighting - Colour consultancy - Project management
- Landscaping

COMMERCIAL

Our interior architects have specialised experience in retail, commercial and hotel design. We are very happy to work as part of a wider professional team, or to undertake individual projects. Our services include:
- Plan and Elevation
- Electrical and Plumbing Drawings
- Space planning - Design of alterations - Custom joinery design for all types of uses
- Furniture specifications and layouts - Specification of finishes, fixtures, fittings
- Lighting - Colour consultancy - Project management
- Landscaping

HOSPITALITY

Our interior architects have specialised experience in hospitality design. We are very happy to work as part of a wider professional team, or to undertake individual projects. Our services include:
- Plan and Elevation
- Electrical and Plumbing Drawings
- Space planning - Design of alterations - Custom joinery design for all types of uses
- Furniture specifications and layouts - Specification of finishes, fixtures, fittings
- Lighting - Colour consultancy - Project management
- Landscaping

CHOOSE CAREFULLY

Choosing an interior architect or designer is a crucial part of your project. You need to be sure that you and your designer can work well together, and also feel confident that he or she will be able interpret your wishes correctly to give you the finished result you want.
